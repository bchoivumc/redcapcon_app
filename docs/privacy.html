Privacy Policy

REDCapCon Mobile Application

Simple Summary: The REDCapCon Mobile Application does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All information is stored locally on your device and never leaves your phone.

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

The REDCapCon Mobile Application ("the App") is designed with your privacy as a top priority. We believe in transparency and want you to understand exactly how the App works with your data.

What Data We Do NOT Collect

The REDCapCon Mobile Application does not collect, store, transmit, or process any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Device identifiers or unique device information
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Crash reports or diagnostic information
  • Cookies or tracking technologies
  • IP addresses or network information
  • Any personally identifiable information of any kind

How the App Works

Read-Only Schedule Data

The App retrieves conference schedule information from a publicly available source (GitHub Pages) to display session times, speakers, locations, and descriptions. This data is read-only and contains no personal information.

Local Storage Only

When you save sessions to "My Schedule" or customize notification settings, this information is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to any server
  • Is not accessible to the app developers
  • Is not shared with any third parties
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the app

Offline Functionality

The App is designed to work offline after the initial schedule download. Once you've loaded the schedule, you can browse, search, and manage your personal agenda without an internet connection.

Local Notifications

If you enable notifications, the App uses your device's local notification system to remind you about saved sessions. These notifications:

  • Are scheduled and displayed entirely on your device
  • Do not require any server communication
  • Can be disabled at any time in the app settings
  • Are managed by your device's operating system

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The only external connection made is to retrieve the public conference schedule from GitHub Pages, which does not involve any personal data.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The App is designed to be completely privacy-friendly for all users.

Data Security

Since no data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of data breach or unauthorized access to your information through the App. Your saved sessions and preferences remain secure on your device, protected by your device's own security measures.

Changes to This Policy

If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will update this page and the "Last Updated" date below. However, given the App's privacy-first design, we do not anticipate significant changes.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no data for you to access, correct, or delete from our systems. You maintain complete control over any locally stored preferences by using the App's built-in settings or by uninstalling the App.
