Emotional Well-being of REDCap Administrators

Jason Lones, Lindsay BreslinREDCap Administrators grapple with a myriad of challenges arising from institutional and local research community dynamics. Their responsibilities span from optimizing user experience and ensuring regulatory compliance to meeting institutional demands and managing the technical aspects of REDCap. The frequent realignment of priorities and roles, task-switching, among REDCap Administrators exacerbates these challenges. This session aims to serve as both a platform for sharing strategies in navigating the unique stressors of our roles as REDCap Administrators and a forum for interest gathering, potentially leading to collaborative projects within the Consortium.
